Who We Are

Our Purpose & Vision

Rooted in faith, led by love — united in our mission to serve Christ and our community.

🕊️

Our Mission

To spread the love of Christ to fellow youths around the world — helping them understand the importance of knowing and serving Christ at a young age. We build a community where faith, friendship, and service flourish together in the heart of Sinai.

🌟

Our Vision

To win more souls to Christ through the spread of the Gospel and by sharing what we have with others. We envision a bold generation of young Catholics in Nairobi who are compassionate, prayerful, and deeply rooted in their Catholic faith.

Meet Our Chaplain

Father Barnabas Nyakundi "Chinendu" is the Youth Chaplain at St. Mary's Parish, Mukuru. Under his compassionate and inspired leadership, our youth group has grown in numbers, in faith, and in its commitment to community service.

He guides our young members with wisdom, patience, and a deep love for the Gospel — challenging every youth to live out their Catholic faith boldly, joyfully, and with purpose in today's world.
